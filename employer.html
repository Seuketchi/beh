  1. Always conduct interviews in safe, public locations or via video calls before inviting someone to your home.
  2. Verify identity documents and check references thoroughly.
  3. Clearly communicate job expectations, duties, and compensation.
  4. Consider a trial period before making a long-term commitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are workers verified on your platform? +

All workers on our platform undergo a basic verification process that includes ID verification, reference checks, and skills assessment. We also offer optional enhanced background checks for added security. Our verification process helps ensure that you're connecting with reliable domestic workers.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service? +

If you're not satisfied with the service provided, we encourage open communication with the worker to resolve any issues. If problems persist, you can report concerns through our platform. For jobs booked with secure payment, we offer a satisfaction guarantee and can assist with conflict resolution.

How do I determine fair compensation? +

Our platform provides current market rate information based on job type, location, and experience level. We encourage fair compensation that aligns with local standards and reflects the worker's skills and experience. You can also view the rate expectations on worker profiles to help guide your offer.

What should I do if I have concerns about a worker? +

If you have concerns about a worker, you can use our reporting system to notify our support team. We take all reports seriously and will investigate the matter promptly. Depending on the nature of the concern, we may temporarily suspend the worker's profile while investigating. Your safety and satisfaction are our priorities.
