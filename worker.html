Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join as a worker? +

Registration on our platform is completely free for workers. We don't charge any fees to create a profile, apply for jobs, or communicate with potential employers.

How long does verification take? +

The verification process typically takes 1-3 business days after you've submitted all required documents. You'll receive email notifications about your verification status.

Can I work part-time through the platform? +

Yes! Our platform caters to various work arrangements. You can find full-time, part-time, or one-time job opportunities based on your availability and preferences.

What if I have a problem with an employer? +

Your safety and well-being are our priorities. If you encounter any issues with an employer, you can use our reporting system to file a complaint. Our support team will investigate and take appropriate action. We also provide resources and guidance for handling difficult situations.
